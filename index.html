Short Bio

I began teaching in Trenque Lauquen (Argentina), my first lesson was in 1992. A year after entering music school in 1994 I began teaching privately in Buenos Aires. Shortly after moving to New York, I began teaching guitar. In 2007, Elefante Music school, in New Jersey, hired me as one of its guitar teachers; I held that job until I moved to Tokyo in 2010. Since 2011, I’ve been teaching guitar, general music, and also coaching students who wants to sing while playing the guitar. My teaching is spread out around Tokyo, Kawasaki, Chiba, and Yokohama.
